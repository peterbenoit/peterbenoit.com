About the Maps

The charts on this page are designed to provide a visual representation of various public health datasets. Each chart is interactive, allowing you to explore the data geographically and gain insights into regional trends.

The data is sourced from data.cdc.gov, and we use libraries like Leaflet for rendering the charts.
