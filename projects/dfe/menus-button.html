Button Menus

How to use

  • Button behaves like a standard outline button - use one of the 3 button colors
  • Used for presenting a list of actionable items or navigable items
  • Should be used to present a list of a few options and not get unnecessarily lengthy
  • Keep menu item copy to a few words or short phrases, avoid full sentences and keep it short/concise
  • Menu items can be accompanied by an icon (optional), but only on the left
  • On hover, menu background will turn light blue
